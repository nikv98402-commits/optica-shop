# Brand-Inspired Design Shotgun Round

Status: ready for visual review  
Branch: `codex/premium-ui-redesign-plan`  
Date: 2026-06-18

## Brief

Create three fresh ViLu directions inspired by the design principles of:

1. Apple: calm, product-first, spacious, precision and trust.
2. Spotify: energetic, bold, editorial panels, strong rhythm and color.
3. The New York Times: editorial authority, serif/gothic tension, dense but credible information hierarchy.

These are inspiration directions only. Do not copy logos, layouts, exact assets, or protected brand systems. The output must remain a ViLu design exploration.

## Variant A — Apple-Inspired Precision

Mood: polished, quiet, high-trust, product object as hero.

Typography:

- System/SF-like sans stack.
- Large but controlled headline.
- Calm supporting copy.

Visual system:

- White/silver surfaces.
- Soft glass panels.
- Minimal borders.
- Product-state preview as a device-like optical card.

Best for:

- trust;
- premium feel;
- lowering the current amateur/noisy impression.

Risk:

- may become too sterile for a consumer eyewear product if not warmed with frame imagery.

## Variant B — Spotify-Inspired Energy

Mood: bold, modern, high-energy, memorable.

Typography:

- Sharp geometric sans.
- Big expressive headline.
- High-contrast labels.

Visual system:

- Deep black base.
- Acid green / amber / off-white panels.
- Playlist-like cards for saved frames and steps.
- Strong CTA rhythm.

Best for:

- freshness;
- younger audience;
- making ViLu feel less like traditional optics.

Risk:

- can feel too entertainment-like unless trust and privacy copy remain strong.

## Variant C — NYTimes-Inspired Editorial Authority

Mood: expert, credible, intellectual, slightly gothic/editorial.

Typography:

- Serif editorial headline.
- Gothic masthead accent.
- Dense but controlled article/card grid.

Visual system:

- Newsprint/off-white surface.
- Black ink.
- Fine rules.
- Score and salon checklist presented like a practical editorial guide.

Best for:

- Knowledge Base;
- Face-fit methodology authority;
- GenEO/source credibility.

Risk:

- may feel less app-like and slower for the try-on flow if overused.

## Recommendation

For the actual product UI, use **Apple-inspired Precision** as the base, add selected **Spotify energy** only to CTA and saved-selection moments, and use **NYTimes editorial authority** for Knowledge Base / Face-fit methodology pages.

Do not choose a single brand inspiration for the entire site. ViLu needs a hybrid:

- Home/try-on: Apple precision + product cockpit.
- CTA/saved flow: Spotify energy, used sparingly.
- Knowledge Base: NYTimes editorial authority.
